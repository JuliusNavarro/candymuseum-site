Mallow Land

Marshmallow room and its thousands of imitation marshmallows hanging from the ceiling. Isn’t it pretty? It looks almost like snow or a bunch of white fat fairies.

Donut Land

One of the museum’s most impressive room. You can either walk down the steps or slide into a sugar rush Hundreds of candy-sprinkled glazed donuts hang from this room’s ceiling.

Cotton Candy Land

This was probably one of the dreamiest rooms at the museum. We have these giant pink cotton balls made to look like cotton candy trees and clouds. The decor here is more fragile!
